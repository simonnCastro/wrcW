WRC Stages

High-grip asphalt stages where precision, braking stability and cornering line are rewarded. Often fastest stage times, but unforgiving to mistakes.

  • Grip: High
  • Setup: Lower ride, stiffer suspension

Loose, changing surfaces demand car control and confidence. Ruts and dust make lines unpredictable; tyre choice and suspension travel are critical.

  • Grip: Medium - variable
  • Setup: Softer suspension, higher ride

Low traction, requiring studded tyres and delicate throttle work. Snow stages reward smooth technique and brave commitment over raw power.

  • Grip: Low
  • Setup: Studded tyres, careful damping
